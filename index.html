Enterprise Resource Planning is used to manage the business. ERP is a system which helps in improving the efficiency of business processes. Similar to CRM, ERP allows to quick collaboration for data throughout all departments within your organisation. When employees start entering data into the ERP system, it happens in real-time, so that everyone has access to that data.

If there is a problem in any are, it will create automatic alerts so you can rectify the issue. Allowing for your departments to begin planning for issues before they become a problem. By allowing the business to focus on the data, instead of the operations, ERP is providing a method to streamline business processes for your organization.
